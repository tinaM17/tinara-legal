Welcome to Tinara, a women's wellness application developed by Tina Majumder. We are committed to protecting your personal health information. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your rights over your data.

SECTION 1

Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Account information — your name, email address, and password
  • Body information — weight, height, and date of birth (to calculate personalised goals)
  • Menstrual cycle data — last period date, cycle length, period length, flow level, and symptoms
  • Daily health logs — water intake, meals, calorie info, exercise, sleep hours, and mood
  • Goals and preferences — wellness goals selected during onboarding
  • AI chat messages — messages you send to the Tinara AI companion

Information collected automatically

  • Device information — device type, operating system version
  • Usage data — features you use and how often you open the app
  • Crash reports — technical information to help us fix bugs

Information from Google (if you sign in with Google)

  • Your name and email address from your Google account
  • Your Google profile photo (if available)
  • We do not receive your Google password

🚫 We NEVER use your data for advertising, selling to third parties, or building advertising profiles.

SECTION 3

AI Chat and Data Processing

Tinara includes an AI companion powered by Claude (provided by Anthropic, Inc.). When you send a message:

  • Your message and cycle context are sent to Anthropic's servers to generate a response
  • Anthropic processes this data per their own privacy policy (anthropic.com/privacy)
  • We do not store your full chat history beyond the current session
  • Chat content is not used to train AI models without your explicit consent
SECTION 4

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase with industry-standard security practices.

  • All data is transmitted using HTTPS encryption (TLS 1.2 or higher)
  • Your password is hashed and never stored in plain text
  • Health data is protected using row-level security — only you can access your own data
  • We do not store your Google login credentials
  • API keys and secrets are stored securely server-side

✅ If you delete your account, all your personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days.

SECTION 6

Your Rights

Right to access

View all your data inside the app at any time — your profile, cycle logs, and health data are always visible to you.

Right to correction

Update your information at any time via Settings → Edit Profile.

Right to deletion

Delete your account from Settings → Edit Profile → Delete my account, or email us. All data is permanently deleted within 30 days.

Right to data portability

Request a copy of your data by emailing us. We will provide it in a readable format within 30 days.

🇮🇳 Indian users: You have rights under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP) 2023, including the right to know, correct, and erase your data.

SECTION 7

Children's Privacy

Tinara is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has created an account, contact us immediately and we will delete it.

SECTION 8

Medical Disclaimer

⚠️ Tinara is a wellness tracking app and is not a medical device. Cycle predictions, AI responses, and health tips are for informational purposes only and are not medical advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical concerns, especially regarding irregular periods, pregnancy, or any symptoms that worry you.

Questions or Concerns?

We aim to respond to all privacy-related requests within 30 days.
