## Introduction

Welcome to Tinara ("we", "our", or "us"). Tinara is a women's wellness application developed and operated by Tina Majumder. We are committed to protecting your personal health information and your privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and what rights you have over your data. By using Tinara, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the app.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information you provide directly

When you create an account and use Tinara, you may provide:

- **Account information** — your name, email address, and password
- **Body information** — your weight, height, and date of birth (used to calculate personalised calorie and water goals)
- **Menstrual cycle data** — your last period date, cycle length, period length, and period logs including flow level and symptoms
- **Daily health logs** — water intake, meals and calorie information, exercise activity, sleep hours, and mood
- **Goals and preferences** — wellness goals you select during onboarding and in settings
- **AI chat messages** — messages you send to the Tinara AI companion

### 1.2 Information collected automatically

When you use the app, we automatically collect:

- **Device information** — device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
- **Usage data** — features you use, screens you visit, and how often you open the app
- **Crash and error reports** — technical information to help us fix bugs and improve stability

### 1.3 Information from third parties

If you sign in with Google, we receive from Google:

- Your name and email address as registered with your Google account
- Your Google profile photo (if available)

We do not receive your Google password.

## 3. AI Chat and Data Processing

Tinara includes an AI companion powered by Claude (provided by Anthropic, Inc.). When you send a message in the AI chat:

- Your message, cycle phase information, and daily stats are sent to Anthropic's servers to generate a response
- Anthropic processes this data according to their own privacy policy (available at anthropic.com/privacy)
- We do not store your full chat history on our servers beyond the current session
- Chat content is not used to train AI models without your explicit consent

---

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### Where your data is stored

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (a cloud database provider). Data is stored on servers located in data centres with industry-standard security practices.

### How we protect your data

- All data is transmitted using HTTPS encryption (TLS 1.2 or higher)
- Your password is hashed and never stored in plain text
- Your health data is protected using row-level security — only you can access your own data
- We do not store your Google login credentials
- API keys and secrets are stored securely and never included in the app code

### How long we keep your data

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will permanently delete all your personal data within 30 days.

## 6. Health Data — Special Protections

Tinara handles sensitive health information including menstrual cycle data, symptoms, mood, and body measurements. We treat this data with extra care:

- We never share your health data with employers, insurance companies, or advertisers

## 9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

### Right to access
You can view all your personal data inside the app at any time — your profile, cycle logs, and health data are visible in the respective screens.

### Right to correction
You can update your personal information at any time via Settings → Edit Profile.

### Right to deletion
You can delete your account from Settings → Edit Profile → Delete my account, or by emailing tinamajumder1720@gmail.com. All your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

### Right to data portability
You may request a copy of your data by emailing tinamajumder1720@gmail.com. We will provide it in a machine-readable format within 30 days.

### Right to withdraw consent
You may stop using the app and delete your account at any time. Deleting your account withdraws all consent you have given.

### For users in India (Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023)
As an Indian user, you have the right to:
- Know what personal data we hold about you
- Correct inaccurate data
- Erase your personal data
- Nominate someone to exercise your rights on your behalf

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at tinamajumder1720@gmail.com.
